# Product Guide: H Brothers Website Redesign

# Initial Concept
redesigning the Landing page to see if they want to pay me "Joe's Tech Solutions" for the update and the SEO optimized site with some help for Marketing.

## 1. Project Overview
This project is a high-performance redesign of the landing page for **H Brothers**, a beloved comfort food restaurant in Downtown Escondido. The goal is to modernize their online presence to drive foot traffic and online orders, while showcasing the technical expertise of **Joe's Tech Solutions**.

The site serves as a proof-of-concept to demonstrate improved SEO rankings, ADA compliance, and mobile responsiveness, positioning Joe's Tech Solutions as a valuable partner for their digital marketing and technical needs.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Local Residents:** Escondido locals seeking high-quality comfort food for lunch or dinner.
*   **Tourists:** Visitors to Downtown Escondido looking for highly-rated, authentic dining experiences.
*   **Loyal Patrons:** Existing customers checking for weekly specials (e.g., Brisket Mac & Cheese) and operating hours.

## 3. Key Features & Functionality
*   **Visual Menu Showcase:** High-quality imagery of signature dishes (Burgers, Poutine, Mac & Cheese) to drive appetite appeal.
*   **Menufy Integration:** Seamless linking to their existing Menufy backend for online ordering (Pickup/Delivery), ensuring operational continuity.
*   **AI Concierge (Gemini LLM):** A smart assistant powered by Google Gemini API to answer customer queries.
    *   *Handover Strategy:* **Client-Owned API Key.** The system is architected so H Brothers can provide their own Google Cloud API key, ensuring direct billing and ownership of their AI usage.
*   **Local SEO Optimization:** Strategic content and structure (Schema markup, localized keywords) to rank #1 for Escondido comfort food searches.

## 4. Brand Identity & Design Principles
*   **Hearty & Homestyle:** The design reflects the warm, family-owned atmosphere of the physical location.
*   **Local Authority:** Prominently features their "Local Legend" status and high ratings to build trust with new visitors.
*   **Modern & Responsive:** A mobile-first, lightning-fast experience that demonstrates the technical superiority of the new site over typical restaurant templates.
